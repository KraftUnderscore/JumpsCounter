JumpsCounter

The best Android application to count your jumps out there. Tracking your skipping rope training was never easier!

  • Learn more
  • Source Code

Jumps counter, obviously

Using advanced acceleration sensors inside Android device the JumpsCounter app can provide somewhat accurate results.

Session summary

Get results immediately after finishing your amazing jumping session! See how many jumps you did, how long were you jumping and your average jumping speed. There's even a share button provided to make it easier to boast to your friends!

Plenty of statistics

With one touch you can get access to your all-time statistics. See your best single session results as well as total summaries.

Technologies used

Java

All functional code was written in Java.

Room database

Room was used for communication with the underlying SQLite database.

Sensors

The app relies heavily on the usage of acceleration sensor.

Modern UI

Modern and optimized UI thanks to use of Fragments.
